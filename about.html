About Us

“Melbourne Public Library is an institution dating back to 1929. We have a wide range of resources available, including books, CDs, DVDs, magazines, and an extensive collection of downloadable content.”

Our History

Founded in 1929, Melbourne Public Library has been serving the community for over 90 years. From humble beginnings, it has grown into a beloved resource for education, entertainment, and community engagement. Our mission is to foster a love of reading, learning, and curiosity for people of all ages.

Our Services

  • Books, Audiobooks, and eBooks
  • CDs, DVDs, and Blu-ray Rentals
  • Magazines and Journals
  • Downloadable Content: E-learning and Audio
  • Community Programs and Events
  • Free Wi-Fi and Computer Access
  • Study Rooms and Quiet Spaces

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire lifelong learning, foster creativity, and provide a space where everyone can find something that sparks their curiosity. We strive to offer quality resources, accessible programs, and an inclusive environment for our diverse community.

Our Vision

We envision a future where Melbourne Public Library is at the heart of the community, where individuals of all backgrounds and interests come together to learn, grow, and connect.
